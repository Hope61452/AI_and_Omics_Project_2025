# AI and Omics Research Internship (2025)

This repository serves as the central storage for research projects and computational workflows completed during the **AI & Omics Research Internship**. 

The primary focus of this work is the application of bioinformatics tools to solve complex biological problems, with a specific emphasis on high-throughput data analysis and clinical transcriptomics.

## 🧬 Primary Project: Microarray Analysis of Lung Cancer (GSE31547)
This project involved the comprehensive transcriptomic profiling of the **MSKCC-A Primary Lung Cancer Specimens** dataset. 

### Key Computational Workflow:
* **Data Acquisition:** Retrieval and processing of 154 primary lung adenocarcinoma specimens from the Gene Expression Omnibus (Accession ID: **GSE31547**).
* **Data Pre-processing:** Normalization and quality control of high-density expression profiles using **R**.
* **Differential Expression Analysis:** Utilizing the `limma` package to identify statistically significant gene expression signatures in primary lung cancer specimens.
* **Functional Interpretation:** Statistical analysis of expression patterns to identify potential diagnostic or prognostic biomarkers.
* **Tools Used:** R, Bioconductor (GEOquery, limma, affy), and ggplot2 for visualization.
